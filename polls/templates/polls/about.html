WISQER

About

Wisqer is a social media question-and-answer website that provides curious individuals with a platform to get answers to their questions.
Wisqer was named after cat "whiskers," which are known to indicate curiosity, along with other emotions and intentions.

How to get started

You can freely view the site without logging in!
However, if you want to create questions, replies, and interact with other functionalities, you must log in or register. Registering a new account requires email verification.

Features to know!

Wisqer is packed with features. Once you log in, you can like and bookmark posts, follow users, edit your questions, search for questions, and more!
For those not logged in, you can still view all posts, search for questions, and view other users' accounts.

Development

Most of the development was done during college semesters. Wisqer began in early September 2024 and is now close to completion in March 2025.
The site has also been deployed to two different cloud platforms: DigitalOcean and AWS. Wisqer is currently being hosted on AWS.
